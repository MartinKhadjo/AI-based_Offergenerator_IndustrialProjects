# Engineering Boundaries

This repository is intentionally designed as a **showcase repository**, not as a source-release repository.

## Included
- product overview,
- architecture documentation,
- workflow documentation,
- extracted diagrams from the official manuals,
- public-safe operational notes,
- evidence of engineering scope.

## Excluded
- private source code,
- internal implementation details,
- full prompt engineering logic,
- template internals beyond high-level description,
- proprietary business logic,
- deployment secrets,
- customer or internal data.

## Why this boundary exists
The goal is to make the engineering value visible to employers and evaluators while preserving intellectual property and reducing the risk of misuse or direct copying.
